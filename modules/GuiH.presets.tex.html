Functions

preset.checker (...)
Makes a checkerboard texture with any amount of colors

Parameters:

  • ... number colors to make the texture from

Returns:

    table guih.texture

Usage:

    local GuiH = require("GuiH")
local texture = guih.presets.tex.checkerboard(colors.white,colors.black,colors.gray)
print(texture)
